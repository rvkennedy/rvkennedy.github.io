Detailed Description

The namespace for the clouds library.

The namespace corresponding to the Clouds module. library: (SIMUL)/lib/(PLATFORM)/(COMPILER)/(ConfigurationName)/Clouds.lib The Clouds library depends on the Base, Math, Sky, and Meta libraries.
