Define the grid of pseudo-random numbers to be used in the PerlinNoise3D function. The parameter freq is the frequency, or grid-size. /p RandomSeed is a reproducible seed for the pseudo-random number generation. /p octaves is the number of octaves of noise super-imposed. persistence is the relative scaling of subsequent octaves - higher persistence leads to a more noisy function.
