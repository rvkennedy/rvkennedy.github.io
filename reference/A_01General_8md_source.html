1 Licensing {#licensing}
2 ===========
3 Set your licence key with:
4 
5  simul::base::SetLicence("XXXXXXXXX");
6 
7 - best done before creating your Environment object.
8 There are some useful functions for licence testing, they are in Simul/Base/EnvironmentVariables.h:
9 
10 You can retrieve the set licence with:
11 
12  const char *licence=simul::base::GetLicence();
13 
14 You can check licence validity with
15 
16  const char *result=simul::base::GetLicenceValidityText(licence);
17 
18 You can get an enum result with
19 
20  simul::base::FeatureLevel f=simul::base::GetFeatureLevel();
21 
22 Or retrieve information on the specified licence key:
23 
24  simul::base::LicenceInfo licenceInfo=simul::base::GetLicenceInfo( *lic);
